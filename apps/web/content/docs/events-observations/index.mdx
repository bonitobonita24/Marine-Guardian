# Events & Observations

This section covers the operational records Marine Guardian pulls in from the field: **events** (incidents and monitoring reports) and the **subjects & observations** that describe what is being tracked. It brings together three related surfaces:

- **Event Management** (`/{tenant}/events`) — the Operations List of every event, with filters, search, states, and export.
- **Observations** (`/{tenant}/observations`) — the recorded sightings/position records.
- **Subjects** (`/{tenant}/subjects`) — the tracked entities (boats, rangers, marine animals) observations belong to.

Events are backed by the `event` and `eventType` routers; subjects and observations by the `subject` and `observation` routers.

## Who uses this section

- **Command center operators** triage new events, update their state, and export data.
- **Field coordinators** fill in missing event details, review completed operations, and manage accompanying rangers.
- **Site administrators** oversee the full record and can resolve events in bulk.

Events require the `events` permission (view/write/update/delete); subjects and observations require **view** on the `subjects` feature.
