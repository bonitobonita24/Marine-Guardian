# Subjects

A **subject** is an entity Marine Guardian tracks: a patrol boat, a ranger, or a marine animal. Subjects are organized into **subject groups** and accumulate **observations** over time. The Subjects page at `/{tenant}/subjects` is where you browse and inspect them.

It is backed by the `subject` router; each subject's sightings come from the `observation` router.

## Who uses this section

Operators and field coordinators reviewing what is being tracked and its recent history. Requires **view** on the `subjects` feature.

## Browsing subjects

`subject.list` powers the subject browser with:

- **Search** by name.
- **Filter** by group (`groupId`) or by active status (`isActive`).
- **Infinite scroll** via cursor pagination (up to 50 per page by default).

Subjects are listed alphabetically by name, each showing its group.

## Subject groups

`subject.groups` returns the visible subject groups, each with a count of how many subjects it holds. Groups are the main way to organize and filter subjects — both here and on the [Live Map](/docs/live-map/subject-tracking).

## Subject detail

Selecting a subject opens its detail via `subject.getById`, which includes:

- The subject's **group**.
- Its most recent **observations**, newest first.

## Key actions

1. Open the Subjects page and search or filter by **group** / active status.
2. Select a subject to view its **group** and recent **observations**.
3. Follow a subject onto the **[Live Map](/docs/live-map/subject-tracking)** to see its position.
