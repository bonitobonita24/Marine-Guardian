# Observations

An **observation** is a single recorded sighting or position for a subject — the raw record behind what appears on the map. The Observations page at `/{tenant}/observations` lets you browse these records.

It is backed by the `observation` router.

## Who uses this section

Operators and field coordinators reviewing the history of what was seen and where. Requires **view** on the `subjects` feature.

## Browsing observations

`observation.list` returns observations with:

- **Infinite scroll** via cursor pagination (up to 50 per page by default).
- An optional **`subjectId`** filter to narrow to a single subject's observations.

Observations are ordered by when they were recorded, newest first.

## Relationship to subjects

Each observation belongs to a **subject** (see [Subjects](/docs/events-observations/subjects)). From a subject's detail you can see its most recent observations directly; this page lets you browse observations across subjects, or filter to one.

## Key actions

1. Open the Observations page to browse recorded observations.
2. Filter by **subject** to focus on one tracked entity's history.
3. Scroll to load more (the list pages as you go).
