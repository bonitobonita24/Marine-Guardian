---
title: Sync Status
description: Monitor EarthRanger sync runs and confirm the tenant's data is current.
---

The Sync surface (`/[tenant]/sync`) lets a tenant confirm that its EarthRanger
data is current by reviewing the history of sync runs.

## Who uses this

Available to tenant users who have the `sync` **view** permission. Setting up the
connection and enabling recurring sync remain administrator actions (see
[Connecting EarthRanger](/docs/earthranger-sync/connecting-earthranger)).

## What it shows

- **Sync runs** — a list of recent sync attempts across sync types, newest first.
- **Latest sync** — the most recent run, so you can see at a glance when data was
  last refreshed.

A compact recent-syncs table also appears on the EarthRanger Sync card in
[Tenant Settings](/docs/settings/tenant-settings).

## When sync fails

If EarthRanger becomes unreachable, sync runs are recorded as failed and the
command center surfaces a sync-failure indicator with the last successful sync
time — so operators know the on-screen data may be stale. Resolve the connection
(re-test credentials) and run a manual sync to catch up.
