# Patrol Detail

The patrol detail page (`/patrols/[id]`) shows everything Marine Guardian knows about one patrol and lets you make the small set of edits that survive future EarthRanger syncs.

## Who uses it

- **Rangers, Operators, and Coordinators** — review the full patrol record.
- **Anyone with patrol edit rights** — update the patrol's title, boat name, and area name.

## Header and key details

The header shows the patrol's title, its **state** badge (`open`, `done`, or `cancelled`), and — when they apply — a **Test** badge or a **Deleted** badge, along with the patrol's ID.

A read-only panel summarizes the patrol's key facts:

- **Type** — `foot` or `seaborne`
- **Start** and **End** times
- **First seen** — when the patrol first appeared in a sync

## Edit tab

The **Edit** tab lets you change three fields:

- **Title**
- **Boat name**
- **Area name**

> Fields edited here are protected from future EarthRanger sync overwrites. Marine Guardian records the local edit so a later sync won't undo your change, while leaving every other field to update from EarthRanger normally.

The **Save changes** button stays disabled until you actually change something, and confirms with a short "Saved." message.

## History tab

The **History** tab shows a revision timeline of the patrol's local edits, alongside the original values captured from EarthRanger. Use it to see what changed, and to compare a locally edited field against its source-of-record value. The history is loaded the first time you open the tab.

## Segments

When a patrol is broken into segments, they are listed with each segment's **leader**, **actual start**, and **actual end** times.

## Accompanying rangers

Any additional rangers on the patrol are listed by name — resolved from their registered user account, a known ranger record, or a free-text name, in that order.
