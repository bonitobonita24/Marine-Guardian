# Fuel

The Fuel section tracks the fuel allocated to your patrol operations. Rather than logging every boat trip, it records **fuel allocations** — how much fuel (and at what cost) was received for a given area on a given date — and turns those records into consumption analytics.

## Who uses this section

- **Operators and above** log fuel and edit their own entries.
- **Coordinators and above** edit any entry.
- **Site Admins** delete entries.
- **Everyone with access** can view the entries and the analytics.

## What you can do

- **Log fuel** received for a municipality/area, with liters, total price, and the date received.
- **Review** a chronological table of entries, filtered by area and date.
- **See analytics** — headline KPIs, a consumption trend chart, and a per-area breakdown.

## A note on currency

Each entry records the tenant's **currency at the time it was logged**. If your tenant later changes its configured currency, historical entries keep the currency they were originally recorded in, so past totals stay accurate.
