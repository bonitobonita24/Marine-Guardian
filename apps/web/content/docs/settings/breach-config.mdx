The Breach Register (`/[tenant]/settings/breach`) is an admin-only log for
recording and tracking **data-privacy breaches** through the lifecycle required
by Philippine National Privacy Commission (NPC) rules (Circular 16-03), part of
Marine Guardian's compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

## Who uses this

Restricted to the tenant's administrators (the **tenant owner / Site Admin**
tier). Every action is audited.

## The breach lifecycle

A breach record advances through defined stages —
**detected → assessed → notified → reported → closed** — capturing the required
notifications along the way:

- **Record a breach** — log a newly detected incident.
- **Mark NPC notified** — record that the National Privacy Commission was
  notified (the regime expects notification within 72 hours of knowledge).
- **Mark subjects notified** — record that affected data subjects were informed.
- **Submit report** — record the full written report (expected within five
  business days of the notification).
- **List** — review the tenant's breach register.

## Related privacy surfaces

The breach register is one part of the compliance posture. Self-service
data-subject rights (access, rectification, erasure requests, and portability)
live on the Profile page (`/[tenant]/profile`), and a public privacy notice is
served outside the tenant workspace.
