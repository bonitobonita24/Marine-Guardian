title: Settings
description: Tenant-level configuration — EarthRanger, report branding, and the breach register.
---

Settings holds a tenant's administrative configuration. It is organized into a
few surfaces under `/[tenant]/settings`:

- [**Tenant Settings**](/docs/settings/tenant-settings) (`/[tenant]/settings`) —
  the EarthRanger connection and sync controls for the tenant.
- [**Report Templates**](/docs/settings/report-templates)
  (`/[tenant]/settings/report-templates`) — branded page-header and footer
  configuration for printable report maps.
- [**Breach Register**](/docs/settings/breach-config)
  (`/[tenant]/settings/breach`) — the data-privacy breach lifecycle log.

## Who manages this

These surfaces are for the tenant's administrators (the **tenant owner / Site
Admin** tier, and the platform **Super Admin**). Personal account management —
name, email, password, and data-subject rights — is **self-service** and lives on
the Profile page (`/[tenant]/profile`), not in Settings.
