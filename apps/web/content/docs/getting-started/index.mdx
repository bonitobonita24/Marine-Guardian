Marine Guardian is a web-based **command center for marine protected area (MPA)
enforcement**. It mirrors patrol, event, and subject data from
[EarthRanger](/docs/earthranger-sync) into a live operations view — a War Room,
a live map, an event board, patrol monitoring, fuel logging, and analytics
reports — so operators and coordinators can watch activity, act on incidents,
and produce accomplishment reports.

The platform is **multi-tenant**: every MPA site is its own tenant with its own
users, EarthRanger connection, and data. Tenant workspaces live under a
path-based URL (`/[tenant]/...`), and a platform team manages tenants from a
separate [Super Admin](/docs/super-admin) area.

## Who it is for

- **Command Center Operators** — monitor the War Room and live map, work the
  event board, acknowledge alerts, and log fuel.
- **Field Coordinators** — plan patrol areas, schedule rangers, review completed
  patrols, and generate reports.
- **Tenant administrators (Site Admins / tenant owners)** — manage users and
  custom roles, connect EarthRanger, configure report templates, and handle
  data-privacy tasks.
- **Platform Super Admins** — provision tenant sites and manage platform users.
