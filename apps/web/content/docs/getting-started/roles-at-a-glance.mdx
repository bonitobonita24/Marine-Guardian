---
title: Roles at a Glance
description: The role tiers Marine Guardian uses and what each can do.
---

Marine Guardian uses a **tiered role model**: a platform tier that spans all
tenants, two administrative tiers inside each tenant, and operational domain
roles. On top of these, tenant owners can define **custom roles** with a
fine-grained permission matrix.

## Platform tier

| Role | Scope | What it does |
|------|-------|--------------|
| **Super Admin** (`tenant_manager`) | All tenants (platform) | Provision and manage tenant sites, manage platform users, and enter a tenant to provide support. Managed from the [Super Admin](/docs/super-admin) area. |

## Tenant administration tiers

| Role | Scope | What it does |
|------|-------|--------------|
| **Tenant owner / Site Admin** (`tenant_superadmin`) | One tenant | Full control inside the tenant: [manage users](/docs/users-roles/user-management), build [custom roles](/docs/users-roles/custom-roles), [connect EarthRanger](/docs/earthranger-sync), configure [report templates](/docs/settings/report-templates), and handle the [breach register](/docs/settings/breach-config). |
| **Tenant admin** (`tenant_admin`) | One tenant | Full operational access across the tenant, but **excluded from user management and tenant settings**. This role is also the *ceiling* for any custom role. |

## Operational domain roles

| Role | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| **Field Coordinator** (`field_coordinator`) | Plan patrol areas, schedule ranger assignments, monitor and review patrols, edit event details, and generate/export reports. |
| **Command Center Operator** (`operator`) | Monitor the War Room and live map, work the event feed and Kanban board, acknowledge and escalate alerts, and log fuel. |
| **Viewer** (`viewer`) | Read-only access to the surfaces it is granted. |

## Custom roles

Below the tenant-admin ceiling, a tenant owner can create **named custom roles**
whose access is defined per feature (view / write / update / delete). See
[Custom Roles](/docs/users-roles/custom-roles).

> **Note on naming.** The interface and product materials refer to these roles by
> friendly names (Super Admin, Site Admin, Field Coordinator, Operator). The
> values in parentheses above are the underlying role identifiers used by the
> system.
