Tenant Management (`/admin/tenants`) is where the platform Super Admin onboards
and maintains **tenant sites** — one per MPA. A platform overview
(`/admin`) also surfaces cross-tenant metrics.

## Who uses this

Reserved for the platform **Super Admin** role (`tenant_manager`). Tenant-level
roles cannot create or manage tenants. All actions are audited at the platform
level.

## What a tenant has

- **Name** — the display name of the site.
- **Slug** — the lowercase URL identifier used for path-based routing
  (`/[tenant]/...`). Slugs must be unique; a duplicate is rejected.
- **Timezone** and **currency** — tenant defaults (currency is inherited by
  records such as fuel entries at creation time).

## Key actions

- **List tenants** and view **platform metrics**.
- **Create a tenant** — provide the name, slug, timezone, and currency.
- **Create a tenant with its first admin** — provision the tenant and its initial
  tenant owner (Site Admin) in one step, supplying the admin's email, full name,
  password, and language preference.
- **Update a tenant** — edit its details.
- **Deactivate a tenant** — disable a site; an inactive tenant cannot be entered
  via impersonation.

## Onboarding flow

A typical onboarding: the Super Admin creates the tenant and assigns its first
Site Admin, and the Site Admin then [connects
EarthRanger](/docs/earthranger-sync/connecting-earthranger) and configures the
tenant. If a slug is already taken, choose a different one.
