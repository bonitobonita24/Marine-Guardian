---
title: Users & Roles
description: Manage the people in a tenant and the roles that govern what they can do.
---

Marine Guardian gives each tenant control over **who has access** and **what they
can do**. Two surfaces cover this:

- [**User Management**](/docs/users-roles/user-management) (`/[tenant]/users`) —
  create user accounts, assign roles, reset passwords, activate/deactivate
  accounts, and transfer tenant ownership.
- [**Custom Roles**](/docs/users-roles/custom-roles) (`/[tenant]/settings/roles`)
  — define named, tenant-scoped roles with a per-feature permission matrix.

## Who manages this

Both surfaces are restricted to the tenant's administrators — the **tenant owner
(Site Admin / `tenant_superadmin`)** and the platform **Super Admin
(`tenant_manager`)**. The `tenant_admin` role and every operational role
(coordinator, operator, viewer) **cannot** manage users or roles. See
[Roles at a Glance](/docs/getting-started/roles-at-a-glance) for the full model.
