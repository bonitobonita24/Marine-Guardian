# Managing doodles

Every saved doodle collects on the **Doodles** page, reached from the sidebar under the Interactive Report Map (brush icon) at `/{tenant}/doodles`. It is the shared library of hand-drawn map annotations for your site.

## The list

The page shows one row per saved doodle, with:

- **Name** — the label given when it was saved.
- **Drawn on** — the surface it came from: **Command Center** or **Interactive Report Map**.
- **Created** — when it was saved.
- **Created by** — shown as **You** for your own doodles.

Doodles are **tenant-scoped**: the list shows every doodle saved in your site, drawn by any teammate — not just your own. If nobody has saved one yet, the page explains how to make one with the Doodle tool.

## Previewing a doodle

Click **View** on a row to open a read-only preview. The saved strokes are replayed on a map, framed at the center and zoom the doodle was saved with (or fitted to the strokes for older doodles that predate saved framing). The preview is read-only — there is no drawing or editing here. To change a doodle, redraw it with the Doodle tool and save a new one.

## Deleting a doodle

**Administrators** can remove a doodle with the **Delete** action on its row, which asks for confirmation first. Operators and viewers can open and read doodles but cannot delete them.

## Who can do what

Doodles are governed by the existing **Exports** permission, so there is nothing extra to configure:

- **View the list and preview doodles** — command center operators, field coordinators, viewers, and administrators.
- **Draw and save doodles** — anyone who can create exports.
- **Delete doodles** — administrators (Site Admin tier and above).
