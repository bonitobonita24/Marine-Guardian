---
title: Per-Area Report
description: A PDF report scoped to a single patrol area or protected zone.
---

The **Per-Area** report produces a PDF for one patrol area or protected zone —
the events, patrols, and coverage that fall inside its boundary. It is the go-to
document when a stakeholder asks "what happened in *this* zone?"

## Who uses it

- **Operators, coordinators, and viewers** can generate a Per-Area report of the
  data they already have access to.
- **Site admins / super admins** manage the resulting export jobs (retry,
  cancel, delete) and can also render the PowerPoint variant.

## Key actions

1. Open the Report Map or the Exports screen and choose the **Per-Area** report
   type (stored internally as the `area` report type).
2. Select the target area / protected zone that scopes the report.
3. Pick a paper size — **A4** (default), **Letter**, or **Legal**.
4. Submit. A queued export appears in the history; download it once its status
   turns *ready*.

The export row's summary shows the area the report was generated for, so
repeated per-area reports are easy to tell apart in the history list.
