---
title: Consolidated Report
description: A roll-up PDF that summarizes activity across areas for a period.
---

The **Consolidated** report rolls multiple areas and patrols into a single
period summary — the document you hand to a manager or partner who wants the
whole picture rather than one zone at a time.

## Who uses it

- **Operators, coordinators, and viewers** generate the consolidated PDF.
- **Site admins / super admins** manage the export jobs and can render the
  PowerPoint (`.pptx`) version for presentations.

## Key actions

1. Choose the **Consolidated** report type (stored internally as `consolidated`).
2. Set the reporting scope and period you want summarized.
3. Choose a paper size — **A4** (default), **Letter**, or **Legal**.
4. Submit and download from the Exports history when the job is *ready*.

Because a consolidated PDF can take longer to render than a single-area report,
watch the row's status; admins can **Retry** it if a render fails.
