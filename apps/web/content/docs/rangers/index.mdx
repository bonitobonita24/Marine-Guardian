The **Rangers** area gives every ranger a profile built from real field data.
It answers two questions: *who is on our roster?* and *what has each ranger
actually done* — both the events they **reported** themselves and the ones they
**accompanied** a colleague on.

## Who uses it

- **Coordinators and operators** review individual ranger activity and patrol
  history.
- **Site admins / super admins** additionally manage the known-rangers registry
  (adding manual entries and deactivating rangers).

## What's here

- **[Roster](/docs/rangers/roster)** — the live list of rangers, surfaced in the
  Command Center, and the known-rangers registry behind it.
- **[Ranger Detail](/docs/rangers/ranger-detail)** — one ranger's full profile:
  KPIs, activity timeline, and event summary. Route: `/[tenant]/rangers/[id]`.
- **[Performance Matrix](/docs/rangers/performance-matrix)** — the reported-versus-
  accompanied breakdown that credits each ranger for their work.

## Where ranger data comes from

Rangers are drawn from two sources: those **synced from EarthRanger** and those
**entered manually** in Marine Guardian. When a ranger accompanies another on an
event or patrol, that link is recorded on the event's *accompanying rangers*
relation — which is exactly what powers the accompanied-activity credit
throughout this section.
