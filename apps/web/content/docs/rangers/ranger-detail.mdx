The **Ranger Detail** page is a single ranger's profile, assembled live from
their field activity. Route: `/[tenant]/rangers/[id]`.

## Who uses it

- **Coordinators and operators** review a ranger's activity and patrol history.
- Access is tenant-scoped and permission-checked, so you only see rangers in
  your own tenant.

## What the page shows

- **Profile header** — the ranger's identity at the top of the page.
- **KPI cards** — headline counts of the ranger's activity, including patrol
  KPIs split by **foot patrol** and **seaborne patrol**.
- **Event summary** — the ranger's events grouped by category, split into
  **reported** and **accompanied** (see the
  [Performance Matrix](/docs/rangers/performance-matrix) for how this is counted).
- **Activity timeline** — a chronological feed of the ranger's recent activity:
  events they **reported**, events they **accompanied**, patrols they **led**,
  and patrols they **accompanied**.

## Reported vs. accompanied

The profile deliberately separates two kinds of contribution:

- **Reported** — events where this ranger is the reporter of record.
- **Accompanied** — events and patrols where this ranger was recorded as an
  accompanying ranger, even though someone else was the lead reporter.

Both count toward the ranger's total credit, so support work in the field is
never invisible.
