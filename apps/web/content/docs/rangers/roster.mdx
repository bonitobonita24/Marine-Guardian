The roster is your directory of rangers. It shows who is available and links
through to each ranger's [detail profile](/docs/rangers/ranger-detail).

## Who uses it

- **Coordinators and operators** view the roster to see who is on the ground.
- **Site admins / super admins** manage the underlying known-rangers registry —
  creating manual entries and deactivating rangers who are no longer active.

## Where the roster lives

The live ranger roster is a panel in the **Command Center / War Room** dashboard,
alongside the live map and event feed. From there you open any ranger to see
their full profile at `/[tenant]/rangers/[id]`.

> **Note:** there is no separate standalone roster page at `/[tenant]/rangers`;
> the roster is surfaced within the Command Center, and individual ranger
> profiles live at `/[tenant]/rangers/[id]`.

## The known-rangers registry

Behind the roster is the **known-rangers registry** — the master list of rangers
the system recognizes. Each known ranger carries a **source**:

- **EarthRanger sync** — pulled in automatically from EarthRanger.
- **Manual entry** — added directly in Marine Guardian by an admin.

Admins can **add** a manual known ranger and **deactivate** one that should no
longer appear. These management actions are restricted to admin roles; everyone
else has read access.
